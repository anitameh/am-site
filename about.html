About Anita

Hey there.

Anita does data science and strategy at BlackRock's AI Labs. Before this, Anita was a data scientist at BuzzFeed and Accenture's R\&D Lab, where she focused on machine learning, graph theory, and data-driven journalism. Anita is most passionate about using computational science and artificial intelligence technologies to solve cool and important societal problems, and has a special spot in her heart for Bayesian methods and U.S. K-12 education. Anita's hybrid skillset and full-stack approach to data science have evolved in tandem with the field she loves, something she sometimes writes about.

Originally a California girl, Anita now lives in New York City.
