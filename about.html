About.

Hey there.

Anita is a Data Scientist at BuzzFeed in New York City. Her primary interests lay at the intersection of computational science and social problems, with an emphasis in Bayesian methodology applied to U.S. K-12 education issues. In her spare time, Anita writes for Harvard University’s Institute for Applied Computational Science, volunteers as a teacher at Girls Who Code, and spins. Her favorite language is Python.
